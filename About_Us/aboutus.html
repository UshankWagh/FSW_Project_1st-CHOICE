Our story

Curated interiors for inspired living
The House Of Things is a portal of discovery – showcasing what a home could be. With a passion for authentic, thoughtfully curated design, our offerings range from furniture, lighting, homewares to textiles, curios and art.

Features

Refined

Constantly on the hunt for exquisite and often overlooked design treasures, our team pre-selects each piece - foregoing all and sundry - to cultivate an edited mix of the extraordinary products.

Personal

Backed by a comprehensive understanding of brand and product, our concierge offers unrivalled service – whether it is for sourcing hard to find items or facilitating custom commissions.

Collaborative

Our portfolio remains dynamic and resourceful, as we collaborate with artistic media to create and curate special product as well as content that is engaging and revelatory in its own right.

Proud

All our products are united by exceptional workmanship and provenance. Our partner brands and artists produce, using clean commodities with minimum impact on the environment.

Diverse

Our aesthetics cover an expansive base: from classic to directional or antique to avant-garde - within this varied selection, you will find pieces that complement your unique taste and personality.

Global

Through partnerships with established shippers, you can rely on us for responsive support in overseeing your delivery that fits around your schedule, not ours.
